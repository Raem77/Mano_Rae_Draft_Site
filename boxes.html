Box of memories

Memories can be held, revisited, and preserved through the objects we choose to keep. Over the years, I have collected small fragments of time: photographs, notes, objects, and traces of moments that I did not want to lose. Kept inside boxes, these personal archives became a physical memoir: a way of holding onto experiences, emotions, and details that might otherwise fade.
